Friends Find One Other

Features

Find Friends

True 3D Location Tracking in a 100% Open Source iOS & Android App. Use Facing to locate those you can't seem to find, even though they were right next to you.

Easy to Use

Maps aren't very helpful when you're looking for someone in the same building.  Facing will let you know if you're facing the right way to find who you're looking for.

iOS & Android Integration

Mobile Applications can integrate with Facing using our custom facing:// protocol to allow their users to find one another, or any random point in space.
