Find Friends

You're at a crowded event & even though you're in the same room, you just can't seem to find your friend.  Facing will guide you, whether it's left, right, up or down.

Easy to Use

Maps aren't very helpful when you're looking for someone in the same building.  Facing will let you know if you're facing the right way to find who you're looking for.

Application Integration

Mobile Applications can integrate with Facing using our custom facing:// protocol to allow their users to find one another, or any random point in space for that matter ;)
